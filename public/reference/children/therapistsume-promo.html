Privacy — Non-Negotiable

Client data never
touches the card.

The Therapistsumé credentials the clinician — not the client. Client information is never accessed, never stored, never referenced. The card shows what the therapist knows and has done. Nothing more.

🔒
HIPAA-first by design. Always.
We access only practice-level metadata — your credentials, specializations, continuing education hours, and certifications. We never access, request, or store any client-identifying information. The Therapistsumé shows the therapist's record. Client records stay exactly where they belong — in your hands, under your license, protected by law.

Opt out and we keep nothing. Not anonymized. Not archived. Gone.
