The Framework

Five dimensions.
One profile.

SHAPE is how God has uniquely wired you. The assessment surfaces it. The card makes it portable, shareable, and searchable.

S
Dimension 01
Spiritual Gifts
Special gifts given by the Holy Spirit to build up the church and serve others — Teaching, Mercy, Leadership, Hospitality, and more.
On your card: Teaching · Mercy · Encouragement
H
Dimension 02
Heart
The passions and causes God has placed in your life — what energizes you, breaks your heart, and drives you toward action.
On your card: Youth · Global Impact · Serving the Overlooked
A
Dimension 03
Abilities
Talents, skills, and knowledge developed through life and work — everything from music to logistics to counseling to carpentry.
On your card: Music · Administration · Communication
P
Dimension 04
Personality
How God uniquely wired you to relate to people and approach challenges — introvert or extrovert, structured or spontaneous, detail or vision.
On your card: Introverted · Detail-oriented · Structured
E
Dimension 05
Experiences
The story God has written through your life — the joys, hardships, and turning points that shaped who you are and what you have to offer.
On your card: Recovery · Marriage · Cross-Cultural Living

How It Works

Three steps.
One card.

The assessment already does the work. The card just shows it.

STEP 01
Take the SHAPE Assessment
Westside's assessment walks you through all five dimensions — spiritual gifts, heart, abilities, personality, and experiences. It takes about 20 minutes.
STEP 02
Your card generates automatically
Based on your results, your SHAPE Card is built. Five dimensions. Your gifts listed. Ministry fit suggestions. Assessment-verified badge fires automatically.
STEP 03
Share it anywhere
One link. Your card lives at shapecard.org/[yourname]. Share it with ministry leaders, in your small group, on LinkedIn, or in your email signature.
FOR LEADERS
Search your congregation's SHAPEs
Ministry leaders can search by spiritual gift, ability, or heart. Find who's equipped for what. Stop guessing. Start matching people to purpose.
