The Badge System

Honest by default.
Transparent by design.

The badge tells you where the data came from. Always. No ambiguity. People can rely on it.

✅
AuditForge Verified
Data sourced directly from an AuditForge instance. The schema is the source of truth. Engagement COMPLETED. Controls APPROVED. Hours logged. No manual review required — the system governs the badge.
VERIFIED · AUDITFORGE DATA
🔗
Third-Party Connected
Data imported from TeamMate, Pentana, AuditBoard, Workiva, or ServiceNow GRC. Verified against the connected system. Clearly attributed to the source. Trustworthy — just not AuditForge governed.
CONNECTED · TEAMMATE / PENTANA
✏️
Self-Reported
Manually entered by the cardholder. Clearly labeled on the card. Not hidden, not penalized — just honest. The auditor chooses to show it. The viewer knows what they're looking at.
SELF-REPORTED · MANUAL ENTRY
🔍
Searchable — If You Allow It
Discoverability is opt-in only. If you allow it, your card appears in search results, filterable by Verified or Self-Reported. If you don't allow it, you don't exist in the index. Default: private.
OPT-IN ONLY · DEFAULT PRIVATE

Privacy is not a feature.
It's the foundation.

We don't monetize your data. We monetize features. The data is yours. Always. That's not a policy — that's the product.

🙋
Opt-in data collection only
We collect behavioral signals only if you explicitly allow it. No passive collection. No default-on tracking. You decide what we see — before we see it.
🚫
Zero PII in any aggregate
Any signal we collect is stripped of all descriptive and personally identifiable information before aggregation. We analyze patterns, never people.
🗑️
Opt out means gone. We mean it.
If you opt out, we delete everything. Not anonymized. Not archived. Not "retained for legal purposes." Gone. We don't want data we don't have permission to hold.
🏢
The firm owns the AuditForge data
AuditForge data belongs to the firm that runs it. Ledger reads it to generate your card — with the firm's permission. The auditor owns their Ledger profile. These are separate.
📦
Export anytime, completely
Your Ledger profile is yours. Export it in full at any time — JSON, PDF, or raw data. No lock-in. No "we'll send it within 30 days." Immediately, completely, yours.
💰
We don't sell data. Period.
No data licensing. No advertiser access. No "anonymized insights" sold to third parties. The revenue model is features, not surveillance. That's a business decision, not just an ethical one.
"If we don't have trust, we don't have anything. The data is the auditor's. The badge is earned. The privacy is absolute. That's not a positioning statement — that's how this works."
